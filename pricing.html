Pricing

Fixed pricing for the modern legal team

Pai operates a tiered managed service model designed to deliver predictable costs and scalable efficiency. Choose fixed project fees or one of our subscription models.

Foundation

Foundation

For smaller legal teams beginning their AI journey. Get started with targeted workflow automation and performance reporting — without the complexity of a full rollout.

Includes

  • Workflow setup and initial automation
  • AI tooling configuration
  • Up to 5 document reviews per month by a qualified solicitor
  • Performance reporting
  • Dedicated Pai contact
  • Governance framework basics
Managed

Managed

For mid-sized legal teams requiring a fully managed AI capability. Custom AI assistants, advanced dashboards, and continuous optimisation — all handled by Pai.

Includes everything in Foundation, plus

  • Custom AI assistants built for your workflows
  • Advanced analytics
  • Ongoing workflow optimisation
  • Up to 20 document reviews per month by a qualified solicitor
  • Dedicated legal technologist
  • Full governance and compliance framework
  • Monthly performance reviews
Enterprise

Enterprise

For large organisations requiring end-to-end AI management across their entire legal function. Bespoke models, dedicated specialists, and full compliance assurance.

Includes everything in Managed, plus

  • Bespoke AI models trained on your data
  • Dedicated Pai specialist team
  • High-volume usage (subject to fair use)
  • Full compliance assurance and audit
  • Multi-jurisdiction legal coverage
  • Quarterly executive reviews

All Pai engagements are scoped individually. Pricing is based on the volume of work, the complexity of your legal workflows, and the level of managed service required.

Not sure which tier fits?

Talk to the Pai team. We'll recommend the right starting point for your legal function.
