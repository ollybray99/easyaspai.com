What Pai delivers

Pai accelerates M&A and investment due diligence by deploying AI across entire contract portfolios. Our system extracts change of control clauses, assignment restrictions, termination triggers, liability caps, and material obligations — automatically categorised and surfaced in structured reports. Our deep regulatory expertise means we rapidly identify legal risk within portfolios, giving deal teams the information they need to move fast.
